My adventures

A troublesome hike

4.2 7 ratings expense Rs. 400 Hiking

Hiking to Nuwakot wasn't really easy to do. We peacefully were going on making fun of eachother's exhaustion. Until we ran into a group of snakes and monkeys out of nowhere. We ran with all the might we had until some other visitor saw us and helped us. Together laughing at our situation we moved forward. Around 6pm we reached the top and saw Butwal in the night light. It was so beautiful.

Time in Tansen

3.5 6 ratings expense Rs. 1200 Tika

Tansen as always was nice and cold. The city was filled with the sound of Malshree Dhun and Temple bells. We went to my Dad's elder brother. We went to explore the city of Tansen. The view of mountains and nature was just majestic. Everyone was dressed in traditional costume and dhaka clothes. Some were playing the Malshree Dhun and people were crowded in temple. It was a warm-cold expereince.

Flight to Tulsipur

4.0 7 ratings Speed 100Km/Hr Tika

Since Tulsipur is Mom's home, we go there every year. It is easy life there. We have to do no work, we go there we sit and eat, get a lot of money and return. Half of our earning comes from Danag since there are a lot of places we put Tika on. Dad was flying the car, like he literally drove it like 100km/hr. The lowest he got was maybe 60-80km/hr. Wish you could experience it with me.
